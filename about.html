Guy In a Garage

Welcome, I'm Dave, and this is my garage. Inside this garage I have spent countless hours creating unique pieces that bring out the beauty of natural wood. All the products brought to you are handmade woodcrafts from my garage. No big machines stamping out chunks of wood, that would take all th fun out of it. As with all things handmade, nothing is perfect. Each piece is unique which brings natural beauty and slight imperfections which only adds to the uniqueness of the piece.

Sawdust, AKA man glitter, is therapy.

GIG (Guy In a Garage) Woodcraft started out of a love of sawdust therapy. I've been a public servant for 29 years. With public service comes stress and a need of therapy. There is no better therapy than mind numbing hours spent sanding wood until it shines. Most of what I've built has been kept or given to friends and family. It was those recipients who encouraged GIG over a number of years. Now that encouragement brings GIG to you as I work to bring out a retirement gig by doing what I love to give you something you will love. Be involved and let me know your thoughts, your likes and dislikes, your wood and wood combination suggestions. I look forward to hearing from you.
